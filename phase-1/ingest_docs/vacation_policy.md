# Cumulonimbus Robotics — Vacation Policy

Effective: 2026-01-15

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees at Cumulonimbus Robotics accrue 22 days of paid vacation
per calendar year, prorated for partial-year employment. Vacation accrues at
a rate of 1.833 days per month and is available for use immediately upon
accrual.

## Carryover

Up to 8 unused days may be carried into the following year. Days beyond 8
are forfeited on December 31. There is no cash payout for forfeited days,
except upon termination.

## Blackout Periods

No vacation may be taken during the two-week period leading up to a
hardware shipment milestone, currently scheduled for the third week of
March and the second week of October. Exceptions require approval from
both the engineering manager and the VP of Hardware (currently Priya Rao).

## Sabbatical

After 5 continuous years, employees are eligible for a 6-week paid
sabbatical. The sabbatical does not count against annual leave.
