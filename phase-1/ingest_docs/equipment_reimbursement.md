# Cumulonimbus Robotics — Equipment Reimbursement

Effective: 2026-02-01

## Home Office Stipend

Each employee receives a one-time home office stipend of $1,800 USD upon
hire, with an additional $600 refresh allowance every 30 months. Receipts
must be submitted within 60 days of purchase via the Brex portal.

## Eligible Items

The stipend may be used for: ergonomic chair, sit-stand desk, monitor up
to 32 inches, mechanical keyboard, mouse, webcam, and noise-cancelling
headphones. Standing desk converters count toward the desk category.

## Ineligible Items

The stipend may NOT be used for: gaming consoles, personal subscriptions,
art or decor, smart speakers, or any device with a primary entertainment
function. Tablets are ineligible unless required by role (verified by
manager).

## Approval

Items under $400 are auto-approved. Items between $400 and $1,200 require
manager approval. Items above $1,200 require a written justification and
VP-level sign-off. Submit justifications to ops@cumulonimbus.example.
