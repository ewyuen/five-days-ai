# Cumulonimbus Robotics — On-Call Rotation

Effective: 2026-03-01

## Rotation Structure

The platform team runs a 7-day on-call rotation, Monday 10:00 UTC to the
following Monday 10:00 UTC. There are three rotation tiers:

- **Primary**: first responder, must acknowledge alerts within 5 minutes
- **Secondary**: escalation if primary does not ack within 15 minutes
- **Manager**: final escalation, paged only for SEV-1 incidents

## Compensation

On-call shifts pay a flat stipend of $450 per week (primary), $200 per
week (secondary), and $0 (manager — covered by salary). Page-outs during
weekends or between 22:00 and 07:00 local time pay an additional $75 per
incident, capped at $600 per shift.

## Trades

Engineers may trade shifts up to 72 hours before a shift starts. Trades
must be logged in the #oncall-trades channel and confirmed by the
on-call manager (currently Marcus Liang). Same-day trades require an
incident report explaining the reason.

## Holiday Coverage

Shifts that include a US federal holiday pay a 1.5x multiplier on the
weekly stipend. The Cumulonimbus winter shutdown (December 24-January 1)
is covered exclusively by volunteers at 2x stipend.
